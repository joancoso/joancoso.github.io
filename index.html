About

Chemist by training, programmer by choice. I have spent my career at the intersection of environmental chemistry and computational modeling — building tools that help predict and prevent contamination in drinking water and wastewater systems.

More about me

Research

My work focuses on two interconnected problems: the unintended formation of harmful disinfection byproducts during drinking water treatment, and the incomplete removal of organic contaminants in wastewater. In both cases, I build predictive models that give engineers actionable insight before running expensive experiments.
